THE WALK

The First Poem


I can see the stars in the river, that floats through the sky.
I can see that there is a road ahead, with soft grass growing by its sides
There are trees all around and, a lampost not far away.




The moon is bright, brighter than I've ever seen
And so there is light, more than there ever has been
All of a sudden, in that trance, I hear the trees whisper
That they have seen me somewhere and that my life had been a beezer.

With my tears, came down a leaf - and then many more followed
By my ear when they reached, each of them rang so hallow.

You did it!
You did well
Fear not any longer
What you did was your best back then...
