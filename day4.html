The last of the series.



I narrated a very broken story as one would proceed through the four pages.
I just had this idea and so, I believed that it was a one worth trying.
However, from my side, I would just like to add that what went past was just a story.
It is just the random philosophy of a random guy out there somewhere trying to make stuff work in his way.
We all are bound to live our lives as subject to stuff like calamities, circumstances and furthermore...
CONSEQUENCES...


Ok. I ought to be no one to comment on stuff like that.
I am myself a resident of a country where people and my neighbours are more keen on creating war-like situations
than fighting a war that is already home. And if any person reads this say in 2022 or so, by which I believe that
the war would be over, I speak of the COVID-19 which I sincerely pray that would have had gone by that time.
People, all I wished to say through this blog is that we are stronger, trust me, we are than a burning sensation
of letting go this pain through ones greater and are often described as illnesses in psychological meet-ups.
And for god's sake and for our own sake, at least remember - our actions - positive and negative ring throughout eternity.


That, darling is Physics. Some might just be cursing me at this point, saying that all this for the sake of
f***ing stupid physics!!! Buddy, there are things we ought to know. And one thing I've known is that if I understand
the cause of something, let's say why I love football, I often try to down play that urge to watch a match. The same
for cricket and so forth. One thing we must understand is that this bloody mind of ours is pretty special.
It's a f***ing miracle, people! You know what, you don't necessarily have to be a yogi or a prophet or Jesus to know it.
You just need to be Yourself...
